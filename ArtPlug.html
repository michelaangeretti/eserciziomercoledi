THE ART PLUG POWER HOUSE

The Art Plug Power House 2023 main event will take place at The Citadel over the course of 3 days, where guests were immersed into our high-sensory experiential art fair. Over 60 artists displayed their work through a myriad of activations, installations, and exhibitions. Our motocross performance show continued for 3 more nights — an added bonus of The Citadel was the immersive “Red Zone” shipping container that allowed guests to be in the performance track, getting flipped over just a few feet above. Over 5,000 guests entered the grounds and scoured over 100,000 sq. ft. of Power House chaos until the clock struck midnight on Sunday evening.

ART BIKES ON A ROOF

On December 5, 2023, we will kick-off our Miami Art Week festivities with a historic event on top of Miami Design District’s iconic Museum Garage. In collaboration with Red Bull, we will pull off the world’s first rooftop freestyle motocross show, connecting 4 FMX stuntmen with bikes designed by some of our signature visual artists and creating a “performance art” show for our special guests.
